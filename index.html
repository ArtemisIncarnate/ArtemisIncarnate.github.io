Book Assignment 3: Suspect Report

Communities can help each other be aware of recent crime activity
by reporting incidents, anonymously or otherwise.
The linked map allows community residents to view where crimes
have occured in real time and use the data to stay safe.
The map focuses on the crime on and surrounding the
Loisiana State University campus.

Book Assignment 5: Watershed Sinks in East Baton Rouge Parish

Watershed sinks are needed to help capture stormwater runoff and allow
for underground aquifer recharge. A local map of watershed sinks in
East Baton Rouge Parish was developed with data collected by the
LSU College of Coast and the Environment.

Book Assignment 6: U.S. Population Growth

U.S. Population Growth from 1790 to 1999 is modeled on the time enabled
map below. Users can compare growth between rural and metropolitan
cities over time using the slider on the map. Additionally, users can
compare previous growth trends with current population sizes using
embedded pop-ups, leading to the U.S. Census Bureau Population Clock.

Midterm Project: Web Maps and Web Applications

This BREC Community Parks & Recreation Map allows users to search BREC
locations and their associated amenities in and around Baton Rouge.
Users can plan travel to these faclities on a varity of greentrails
that are displayed on the Web Mapp. Additionally, users can determine
the number of parks and recreation centers within a specified amount
driving or walking time.

URBAN PARKS AND GREENSPACES

Increasing green spaces in urban areas improves health, mental wellness,
air quality, and provides a place for community to gather. However,
many studies show that the development and distribution of these spaces
is disproportionate and often benefits predominately White and/or more
affluent communities, creating environmental justice issues.
